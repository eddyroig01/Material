Problem & Solution

The Sargassum Crisis:

  • $200M+ spent annually on cleanup, yet beaches remain impacted
  • Tourism losses: Destinations lose ~11.6% GDP, 4.25% visitors don't return
  • Health hazard: 1 ton decomposing = 302 kg CO₂ equivalent toxic gases
  • Resource waste: 522,226 tons wasted in Mexico (2018) instead of valorized

Our Solution:

AI-powered autonomous fleet that intercepts sargassum before it reaches shore:

  • ISAC Platform: Autonomous collection vessel with AI navigation
  • ISAC Scout: Support vehicle for interception and redirection
  • SargaScan AI: Predictive tracking and real-time detection
  • Fleet Hub: Centralized web platform for fleet management
